About Borderdoodles

The bordoodle, also know as “borderdoodle”, is a hybrid cross between the two smartest dogs in the world, a purebred Border Collie #1 and a purebred Poodle #2. The average Bordoodle has a shoulder height of 18-20 inches and typically weighs 35-45 lbs. However, Mini Borderdoodles can be much smaller with a height of 15-22 inches and a weight from 25-35 lbs.
